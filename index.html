I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Unversity of Minnesota (UMN) and I am a member of Knowledge Computing lab advised by prof. Yao-Yi Chiang. My research focus on building spatial artifical intelligence and constructing deep learning models on large-set of real-world spatiotemporal data.

Currently, I am working on multi-modal learning to jointly capturing spatial information (e.g., retrieve useful information from real-world imagery datasets) and forecasting fine-grained data by constructing deep learning-based model to explore spatiotemporal dependences.

Previously, I received a M.S. degree in Computer Sciecne from the Purdue University Fort Wayne, advised by prof. Jin Soung Yoo. Also, I was a member of Business Intelligence and Information Management Lab advised by prof. Adolfo Coronado.
